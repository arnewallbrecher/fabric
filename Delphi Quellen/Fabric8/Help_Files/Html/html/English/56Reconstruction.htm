Reconstruction and Simulation of Orientation Data

This program produces a file of orientation data when you click into an empty projection (Simulation) or when you click on data points in a bitmap (reconstruction).

A third application is the simulation of displacement planes (Calculation of MRSS), and the calculation of the direction of theoretical striations (MRSS)on these planes.for arbitrary principal stresses and shape factors of the stress-ellipsoid.
Click in the menu "Method" on one of these options.
