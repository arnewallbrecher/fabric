Data Input from a Bitmap

A picture of the folded layers must exist as a pixel-graphic (*.bmp, *.gif, *.tif, *.jpg, *.wmf, *.emf) in a file or on the clipboard. In order to draw the isogons ,the axial plane of the fold must be in vertical position. First click an a piont which shall become the new centre of the graphic, then press the button "New Centre point". Then click on the hinge point of a folded layer and press the button "1st point" Do the same with the second folded layer. The program will rotate the fold and the two points are now in a vertical position. Instaed of the cursor, a straight line with inclination of 10° with a point on it appears. Drag this line until this point becomes the point of contact with the fold, then press the button "1st point". Do the same with the second folded layer. Now the isogon is plotted, and a line with inclination of 20° appears. Do the same procedure with this tangent. In this way all possible isogons are plotted. When the angle becomes so steep that no Tangent is possible, press the button "end". Open the graphic form with the button "Plot".
