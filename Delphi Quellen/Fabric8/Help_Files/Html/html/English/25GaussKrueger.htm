Gauss-Krueger-Coordinates

These coordinates were created by Carl Friedrich Gauss and Johann Heinrich Louis Krueger: The earth is divided in strips of meridians of 3° width. Each strip connects the south and the north pole. The middle meridian is in the middle of the strip. Each strip is numbered according to the middle meridian.(0°, 3°, 6°,...give the numbers 0, 1, 2,...). The coordinates of a point are given by easting and northing. The easting value is defined by the distance from the middle meridian in meters. (To avoid negative values a value of 500 km is added). Northing is the distance from the equator measured along the middle meridian.
