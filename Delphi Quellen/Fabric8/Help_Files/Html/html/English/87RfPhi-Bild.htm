Rf/φ Method, Data from a Picture

An image (*.bmp, *.gif, *.tif, *.jpg, *.wmf, *.emf) of deformed pebbles or other markers must already exist, or it must be saved on the clipboard. In the menu 'Import picture' click on 'Paste' or 'From a file' and then on the format of the image.  A list with all saved images of this format will appear.
lick on the image you want to analyse.
When the image appeared, click first on the ends of the long axis, then on the ends of the short axis of a pebble or marker.If you made a mistake you can erase the input for the last marker. Click on 'Terminate input when you are through.
