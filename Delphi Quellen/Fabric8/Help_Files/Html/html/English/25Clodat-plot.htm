Significant Separation of Orientation Data, Plot

Significant separation of clusters in the distribution is carried out.

The statistic parameters of the clusters are calculated as shown in the program 8 (Statistics). Sperical aperture and cone of confidence are drawn with the cluster centre (centre of gravity) as centre. In the menu confidence the probability level saved in Fabric.ini can be changed.
In the menu "Colour" the colours of all plot parameters saved in Fabric.ini can be changed. In the menu "Symbol for data point" the plot symbol can be changed.
The menu "Diagram" ist the same as in the other programs.
