Plot of the Projections of the Globe

In the menus "Projection" and "Colour"‚ the settings in Fabric.ini can be changed.
The distance between the great circles is written into the input field on the right. The value should be a divisor of 90. The 'increment' is the distance between the the calculated points with which the circles are plotted. It should be a divisor of the distance between the circles. The tilt angle is the inclination of the N-S-axis.
