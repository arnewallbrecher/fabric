Plot of the Paleo Stresses

The compressional areas are drawn with contour lines in the Lambert projection. These lines separate areas with different amounts of compression (>20%, >40%, >60%, >80%, > 95%).
In the menu "Colour\Contour lines" you can also choose different grades of gray for the plot of contour lines.
In the menu "Contouring methods" further methods for the visualisation of counting densities are offered.
The menu "Smoothing factor" is used only for the Scalar product-method aktiv.
The Menu "Diagram" ist the same as in the other programs.
The calculated values 'maximum' and 'minimum' allow an estimation of the consistency of the data set. In the ideal case the maximum equals the number of data, and the minimum is zero.
