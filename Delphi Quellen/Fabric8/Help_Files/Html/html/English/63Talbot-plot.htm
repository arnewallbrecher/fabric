Talbot-Methode, Plot

In every strain ellipse an elliptical cone with the apex in the centre of the ellipsoid
can be constructed in which the mantle lines do not show elongation or shortening
(lines of no finite longitudinal strain, n.f.l.s.).
In the panel "within the n.f.l.s. ellipse". Click on "Shortening" when shortened
linears are in the n.f.l.s. ellipse, or on  "Extension" when the elongated linears are in
in this ellipse, then click on "OK".
The orientation of the three axes of the strain ellipsoid is calculated as Eigenvectors
(X>Y>Z). By visual estimation of the projektion points you are requested to click on
one end of the long diameter of the n.f.l.s. ellipse, and then  on one end of the short
diameter.
