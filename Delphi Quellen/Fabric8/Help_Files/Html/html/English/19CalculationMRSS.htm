Calculation of  MRSS on a fault plane

A fault plane is simulated on which the theoretical striation direction (MRSS)  for arbitrary orientations of the principal stresses and arbitrary shape factors of the stress-ellipsoid are calculated with Bott's formula.

Calculation of  MRSS:
Choose the projection, on the right side, click on a shape factor, and put in the values of s1 and s3.Now click on a point for which you want to to produce a slickenside plane and the striation on it. Azimuth of dip and angle of dip of the slickenside plane are shown in the list. When you press "Terminate simulation" the striations are added.
