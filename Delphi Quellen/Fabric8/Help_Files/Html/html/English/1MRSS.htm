MRSS (Maximum Resolved Shear Stress)

The direction on a fault plane (slickenside) in which the maximum shear stress is reached. It is supposed that the striations on a slickenside plane have the direction of the MRSS. The relation between the shape factor of the stress-ellipsoid and orientation of the normal to the plane with respect to the principal stress axes and the MRSS is described by the Bott formula.
