Plot of the Statistical Parameters of orientation data:

The settings of the menus "Confidence", "Projection", "Symbols"‚ and "Colours" are saved in Fabric.ini. They can be changed.
The menu "Diagram" contains options to save the plot (within the rectangular frame) as a bitmap, and options to combine the drawn plot with a plot produced with another Fabric8 program. The options 'Plot with a printer', 'Plot into a HPGL file', and 'Plot into a DXF file' produce a vectorgraphic.
These options open a panel (on the right side) in which the plot parameters can be set. These parameters are saved in Fabric.ini. Further options concern the 'Colour of the periphery', the 'Colour of the diagram', the 'Colour of the frame', and the 'Colour of the background'.
The option 'Printer' prints the plot as a bitmap
The following parameters are calculated and presented graphically:

Preferred orientation,
Concentration parameter,
Cone of confidence,
Spherical aperture,
Centre of gravity,
Eigenvalues,
Eigenvectors,
Cylindricity,
Circular aperture.

For the calculation of the probability parameters a probability interval (Confidence) must be set.
The data list and the table of statistical parameters can be printed with the menu "Print list".
The statistics of orientation data is discussed in Wallbrecher (1986).
