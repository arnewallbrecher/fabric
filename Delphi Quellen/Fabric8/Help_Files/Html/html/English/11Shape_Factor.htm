Shape factor

The shape fator (R) is the ratio derived from the magnitudes of the principle stresses. It describes the shape of a stress ellipsoid.

The formula is

                                                            

The meaning of the shape factor can be seen from a dimensionles Mohr diagram:

R= 0.0, when σ2 = σ3;
R = 0.5, when σ2 = (σ1 – σ3)/2;
R = 1.0, when σ2 = σ1
