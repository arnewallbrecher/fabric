Mohr's Circle, Data Input and Evaluation

1.) Input of stress:
s1 and s2 are integer numbers. Q is counted clockwise from  s1.
Each input of values in the three fields must be finished with 'return'. When input is completed, the button 'OK' appears.
Clicking 'OK' produces Mohr's circle.  sn and ss are shown on the panel on the right side of the plot.
Symbols for the points, their colour and seize can be changed with the pull-down menus. Scaling of the coordinates is in tenths of s1 and in tenths or fifths of (s1 -  s2)/2.

2.) Input of finite strain:
First you have to choose the type of input data. Two types are possible:
1) Input of long and short axes of a strain ellipsoid (axes of a deformed former circular marker (Ooid)).
2) Input of elongations:
In case 1) e1 and e2 are calculated with assumption of no volume change.
All inputs must be terminated with 'return'. After input of the angle the button 'OK' appears. Clicking this button produces Mohr's circle. Elongation, shear angle, and shear appear on the panel on the right side of thecplot.

The menu "Diagram" is the same as in all other graphics.
