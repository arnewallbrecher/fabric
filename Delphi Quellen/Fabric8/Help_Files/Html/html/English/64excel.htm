Data Input from an Excel Worksheet

Excel must be closed before you start Fabric8. Make sure that Excel is not listed in the task manager.
Press Ctrl-Alt-Del' to open the task manager, then click  'erase' to delete "Excel.exe" from the task manager.
Azimuth or strike and dip angle can be listed in one or two rows.
Empty cells and cells containing text are allowed they will be overread.
Azimuth and dip must be separated by a sign ("/") when they are listed in one row.
