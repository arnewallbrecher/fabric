Plot of Small Circles

This program draws small circles with the projections used in Fabric8. Data input can be manual or from a file. In the menu "angle" the scaling (1..360∞ or 1..400gon) must be chosen.
In the menu "Compass" you have to choose whether, in manual data, the cone axes of the small circles will be given as azimuth of dip and angle of dip (Clar-Kompaﬂ) or as strike, dip angle and dip direction (Brunton-Kompaﬂ).
