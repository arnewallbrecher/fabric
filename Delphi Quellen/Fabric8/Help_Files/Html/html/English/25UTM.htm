UTM-Coordinates

UTM means 'Universe Transverse Mercator'. The system was introcuced in 1947.Iit is now established worlwide with exception of the polar regions. The coordinates are found in a similar way as Gauss-Krueger-coordinates, but a meridian strip has a width of 6°. The coordinate axes are also called easting and northing. The earth's surface is divided up.into grid squares (UTM-zone fields).
