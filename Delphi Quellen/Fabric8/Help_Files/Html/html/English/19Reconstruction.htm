Reconstruction of a data list  from a diagram

Import a diagram, then click on the projection method by which the diagram was produced in the Menu "Projections". Choose the fabric type. Click on the ends of the EW-diameter of the diagram. When you click a point in the diagram,  its azimuth of dip and angle of dip  are shown in the list. After clicking on all points press the button "Finish reconstruction". Now you can save the data or print them.
