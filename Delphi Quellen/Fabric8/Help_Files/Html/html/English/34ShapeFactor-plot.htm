Plot of the Eigenvalues of Theoretical and Measured Striations

The principal stress axes s1,and s3, found with the dihedra method, are filled in the fields on the right side of the plot area. Now the theoretical striation directions on the measured slickenside planes are calculated as maximum resolved shear stresses (MRSS) for the shape factors R=0.0, 0.1...,1.0. Their eigenvalues are shown in a triangle plot, together with the eigenvalues of the measured striations. The shape factor can be estimated by comparison of the plot of measured eigenvalues with it's neighbours. The method is described in Wallbrecher et al. (1996).
With the menu "Colour"‚ the colours of the theoretical and measured points can be changed.
The menu "Diagram" contains the same options as in all the other plot programs.
