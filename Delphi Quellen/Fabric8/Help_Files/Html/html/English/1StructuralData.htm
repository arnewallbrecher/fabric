Structural Data:

The following measurements can be evaluated:

Measures of planes (azimuth of dip/angle of dip)
Measures of linears (azimuth of dip/angle of dip)
Lineations from arial or satellite images
Displacement data (slickensides, striations, and polarity)
Fold shapes (Isogons, Fourier analysis)
Strain data (Talbot method, Fry method, Panozzo method, Rf/Phi method).
