Determination of the Principal Stress Axes

(Paleo Stress-Ellipsoid)

This program calculates the orientation of the three principal stresses s1, s2 und s3 from slickenside planes and striations on them. The polarity of the striation lineation (hanging block up or down) must be clearly defined in the field. The algorithsm was developed according to the dihedra method of ANGELIER & MECHLER (1976).
Data input is the same as in the other programs.
