Simulation of Plan Strain, Plot

Deformation can be applied to objects, drawn on the screen (lines, quadrangles, ellipses) or to bitmaps. The bitmap can be loaded from either the clipboard or from a file. The format must be *.bmp.

The deformation can be shearing or flattening. The angle of shear is chosen from the scroll box. Gamma (= tangent of the shear angle) is calculated. Input for falttening strain is in percent of vertical shortening. Shearing can be either affin or non affin . Input for non affin deformation is the factor of encreasing deformation.
