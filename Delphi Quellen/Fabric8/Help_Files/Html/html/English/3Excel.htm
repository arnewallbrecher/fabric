Excel File:

Planar or lineation data can also be read from an Excel sheet. You have two options for the tabulation of data: 1) azimuth of dip and angle of dip in a common row. Both values must be separated by a separation key (i.e. „/“). 2) azimuth of dip and angle of dip are written in two separate rows. These roles may also be overwritten with text or contain empty spaces. Fabric8 ignores such areas.
