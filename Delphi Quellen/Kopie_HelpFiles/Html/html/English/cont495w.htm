Floating Contour Intervals:

If you choose floating contour intervals in the scalar product method, you can select the colour spectrum from the HLS-colour circle. The complete spectrum containing all colours from 0∞ to 360∞ is plotted to the right of the diagram. The colour range which shall be applied, is chosen by clicking on the upper and the lower colour.

Change of the smoothing factor may improve the smoothness of the colour transitions.
