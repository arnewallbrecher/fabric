Plot of the Map

This program plots the map with strike and dip symbols for planes and arrows for linears. The map is drawn either with geographic coordinates, Gauss-Krueger, or UTM-coordinates. In the menu 'margins of the map' The frame of the map can be increased or decreased, or the coordinates of the margin can be redefined. The purpose of the latter is to overlay a map, a digital elevation model, or a satellite image. Fill in the coordinates of the margins of this image to make sure that the image will not be distorted. Overlay is done in the menu 'Diagram' either from clipboard or from a file. In the same menu the new map may be saved and/or printed.
