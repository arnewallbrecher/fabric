Plot of the Statistical Parameters of orientation data:

The settings of the menus 'Confidence', 'Projection', 'Symbols'‚ and 'Colours' are saved in Fabric7.ini. The settings can be changed in this form.
The menu 'Diagram' contains options to save the plot (within the rectangular frame) as a bitmap, and options to combine the drawn plot with a plot produced with another Fabric7 program. The options 'plot with a printer', 'plot into a HPGL file', and 'plot into a DXF file' produce a vector plot.
These options open a panel (on the right side) in which the plot parameters can be set. These parameters are saved in Fabric7.ini. Further options concern the 'colour of the periphery', the 'colour of the diagram', the 'colour of the frame', and the 'colour of the background'.

The option ‚Printer' prints the plot as a bitmap
The following parameters are calculated and presented graphically:

Preferred orientation
Concentration parameter
Cone of confidence
Spherical aperture
Centre of gavity
Eigenvalues
Eigenvectors
Cylindricity
Circular aperture

For the calculation of the probability parameters a probability interval (Confidence) must be set.

The data list and the table of statistical parameters can be printed with the menu 'print list'.

The statistics of orientation data is presented in Wallbrecher (1986).
