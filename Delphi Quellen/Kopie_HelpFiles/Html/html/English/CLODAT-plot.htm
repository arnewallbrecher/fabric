Clusters of Orientation Data, Plot:

Significant separation of clusters in the distribution is carried out.

The statistic parameters of the clusters are calculated as shown in the program 8 (Fabric). Sperical aperture and cone of confidence are drawn with the cluster-centre (centre of gravity) as centre. In the menu confidence the probability level saved in Fabric.ini can be changed.

In the menu colour the colours of all plot parameters saved in Fabric7.ini can be changed. In the menu ‚Symbol for data points' the plot symbol can be changed.

The menu ‚Diagram‚ ist the same as in the other programs.
