Fourier-Analysis, Plot

The calculated b1 an b3 coefficients are numbered and plotted in the diagram. The colour can be changed with the menu colour. The list on the right side shows the calculated coefficients b1, b2, and b3. The lines are:

F = chevron folds
D = parabolic folds
C = semi ellipses
A = box folds.

The numbered lines show increasing amplitudes (HUDDLESTON, 1973).
The coloured field represents the range of possible fold shapes.
