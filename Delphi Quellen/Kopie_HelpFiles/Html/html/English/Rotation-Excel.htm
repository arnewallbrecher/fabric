Rotation, Data Input from an Excel Worksheete

Pressing the button "Start" brings the data from the Excel file into the list field (Data) on the left side of the form. The name of the Excel file appears at the bottom of the form.
Fill in the orientation (azimuth of dip / angle of dip) of the rotation axis and the rotation angle (clockwise, looking in the direction of dip of the rotation axis). The rotation starts after clicking the checkbox "OK". The  rotated data appear in the right list field.
Data and rotated data can be saved. The rotatated data can also be printed.
With the menu "Other Methods" you can switch to other programs of Fabric8 to process the data.
The options are:
Plot of data (switches to  program  3),
Plot of great circles (switches to program 4),
Statistics (Switches to program8),
Contour lines (Switches to program 9),
Separation of Submaxima (Switches to program 10).
