Fourier-Analysis, Data Input from a Picture

The coordinates of the quarter fold can be found from a picture by clicking on them.
Click on 'Import image' to open a *.BMP, *.GIF, *.TIF, *.JPG, *.WMF, or *.EMF-file. The graphic may be loaded either from the clipboard (paste) or from a file.
The menu 'adjust image' enables you to rotate the image or to eliminate vergency.
To find the coordinates of the quarter fold click on the inflection point. Click on the hinge. Now click on the 1/3- and 2/3-coordinates (X1 and X2).
