Program 'Calculations':

In the menu 'data input' you choose the drive which contains the data or 'manual' if you want to add new data. In the menu ‚calculations‚ you choose the type of calculation.

The following calculations can be carried out:

Calculation of the intersection line of 2 planes,
Calculation of the angle between 2 planes,
Calculation of the angle between two lineations,
Calculation of a plane from 2 lineations,
Calculation of the normal on two lineations.
Pitch angle from a lineation.
Lineation from a pitch angle.
Azimuth to strike (plane).
Azimuth to strike (linear).
Strike to azimuth (plane).
Strike to azimuth (linear).

For manual input you also have to choose the scale in which the angles have been measured (1..360° or 1..400gon). For data input this information is not necessary, because all saved data have the 1..360° scale.

The options chosen are shown in the line at the bottom. When all options have been chosen, the button 'continue' becomes active. It leads to the next form.
