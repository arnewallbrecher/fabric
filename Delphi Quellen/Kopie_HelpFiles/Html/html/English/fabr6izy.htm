Cone of Confidence:

The reliability of the calculated centre of gravity is described by a cone with the direction of the centre of gravity as cone axis. This is a small circle in the projection. This cone can be calculated for various confidences. Three levels can be set in the menu ‚confidence': (90%, 95% und 99%). A confidence of 99% means that one percent of the data can lie outside the cone. The apical angle of the cone (Θ) is:


n = number of data

p := confidence, (0.1, 0.05 or 0.01).

The cone of confidence is drawn as a small circle around the centre of gravity It's colour is saved in Fabric.ini and may be changed in the menu 'colour of the cone of confidence'.
