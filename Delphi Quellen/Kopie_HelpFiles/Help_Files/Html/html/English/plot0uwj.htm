Plotting of Data

The settings of the menus 'projection', 'symbol' and 'colour' have been saved in fabric7.ini,and may now be changed.

The menu „Diagram“ contains options to save the plot (within the rectangular frame) as a bitmap, and options to combine the shown plot with a plot produced with another Fabric7 program. The options ‚plot with a printer‚, ‚plot into a HPGL file', and 'plot into a DXF file' produce a vector plot.

These options open a panel (on the right side) in which the plot parameters can be set. These parametres are saved in Fabric7.ini. Further options concern the 'colour of the periphery', the 'colour of the diagram', the 'colour of the frame', and the 'colour of the background'.

The option ‚Printer' prints the plot as a bitmap.
