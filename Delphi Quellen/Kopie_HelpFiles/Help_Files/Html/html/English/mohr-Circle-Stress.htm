Mohr's Circle for Stress

For a plane normal to a principal cut of the stress ellipsoid, the program calculates from the principal stresses (s1 > s2) and from the angle Q between the direction of the larger principal stress and the normal to the plane  normal stress and shear stress at the plane (Fig. 1). The results are shown at  Mohr's circle (Fig. 2).
