Measures of Circular Distributions, Results:

The used data are listed and the angular range in which the data are spread is shown. The statistical measures of the distribution are::

  • The Vector sum,

  • the normalized vector sum,

  • the preferred orientation,

  • the vector mean,

  • the Skewness of the distribution,

  • the Kurtosis of the distribution

The measures are calculated for a circular normal distribution which is called von-Mises-distribution (von MISES (1918)).They are described in detail in WALLBRECHER (1986).
