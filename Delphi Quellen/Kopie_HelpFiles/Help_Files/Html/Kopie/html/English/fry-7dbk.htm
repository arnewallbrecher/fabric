Fry-Method, load Bitmap

In this part of the program the coordinates of the centre points of the markers are found. After clicking on 'Start' you are asked to import a bitmap. To do so, click on the menu 'import picture'.

Procedure for the conventional Fry method:

When the picture appeares on the screen, all centres of the markers are clicked one after the other. The centres are found just by visual estimate. The coordinates of these points are listed on the right side.

Procedure for the normalized Fry method:

When the picture appeare on the screen, first the centre of a marker is clicked, then a point of the margin of the marker. The program calculates the radius of the marker from these two data. The centre is found by visual estimate. Continue with all other markers. The coordinates and the radii of the markers are listed on the right side.

The colour of the points can be changed with the option Colour.

Press the button 'terminate input of points' after clicking on the last point. Now the menu 'save points' is active which saves the data in a file.

The button 'Plot' opens the graphic evaluation.
