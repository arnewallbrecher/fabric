Projektion length vs Rotation Angle

Method of the strain-analysis with the projection method (Panozzo, 1984).

The lengths of the projections of polygon borders on a reference line are calculated for the increments of a rotation around 180°. They are plotted versus the rotation angle.


   Fig. 1

This relation is defined by a sinus-graph (Fig. 1). The ratio ΣP(α)max / ΣP(α)min (P = Projection). is the ellipticity of the resulting strain ellipse. The angle which belongs to ΣP(α)max is the plunge (Θ) of the ellipse towards the reference line.
