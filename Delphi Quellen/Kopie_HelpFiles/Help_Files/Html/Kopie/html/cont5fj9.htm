Counting Calotte:

The counting calotte is the test area which is used to find the counting density. It is a calotte of the globe.Its size is defined in percent of the hemisphere.

The size of the counting calotte is given by the apical angle of the cone which is formed by its periphery and the centre of the globe (s. Fig.).

Since the radius of the globe is 1, the surface of the hemisphere 2 π.

Thus the a-percent surface is 2 π a / 100.

The surface of the calotte is 2 π H.

Then H = a/100.
