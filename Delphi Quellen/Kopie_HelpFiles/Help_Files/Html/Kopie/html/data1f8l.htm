UTM-Zone Field

From 180°W to 180°E the earth's surface is devided uo into 60 meridan strips od 6° width. These strips are numbered with zone numbers (No 1 for the strip from 180°W to 174°W, and No 60 for the strip from 174°E to 180°E). The meridan strips are divided up into zone fields from 80°S to 84°N by latitudes in the distance of 8°. These fields are labeled with letters: the southernmost zone has the letter 'C' and the northernmost one has the letter 'X' (The letters I and O are not used in order to avoid confusion with the digits 1 and 0 (s.
